# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical substance.
- Assume good intent; discuss ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Sharing private data without consent.
- Repeated disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors for severe or repeated violations.
